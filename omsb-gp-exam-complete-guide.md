## What is OMSB and Why Does It Matter?

The **Oman Medical Specialty Board (OMSB)** is the regulatory authority responsible for evaluating and licensing medical professionals in Oman. Established to maintain high healthcare standards, OMSB ensures that all practicing doctors meet international medical competency benchmarks.

## OMSB-GP Exam: Format and Structure

Understanding the exam format is crucial for effective preparation. The [OMSB exam](/exams/omsb) has specific characteristics that distinguish it from other licensing exams in the Gulf region.

### Exam Specifications

**Exam Type:** Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)

**Total Questions:** 100 MCQs

**Duration:** 2 hours (120 minutes)

**Passing Score:** 60% (60 correct answers out of 100)

**Question Format:** Single best answer with clinical scenarios

**Delivery Mode:** Computer-based test at authorized Prometric centers

**Exam Language:** English

**Negative Marking:** No negative marking for incorrect answers

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How difficult is the OMSB-GP exam compared to DHA?**

OMSB and DHA have similar difficulty levels. OMSB has 100 questions in 120 minutes while DHA has 150 questions in 165 minutes, giving slightly more time per question in OMSB.

**Q: Can I work in Oman without passing OMSB?**

No, OMSB licensure is mandatory for all medical professionals practicing in Oman, whether in government or private sector.

**Q: How long is the OMSB license valid?**

OMSB licenses are valid for 2 years and must be renewed before expiry with updated documents and CME credits.

**Q: Can I use my OMSB license to work in UAE?**

No, each Gulf country requires its own licensing exam. However, OMSB experience helps when applying for UAE licenses. Use [PrepMCQ](/features) to prepare for multiple exams simultaneously.

**Q: What happens if I fail the OMSB exam?**

You can retake the exam after 30 days. Use the gap to identify weak areas and focus your preparation using [targeted subject practice](/subject-exams).

**Q: Is clinical experience in Oman better than UAE?**

Oman offers excellent clinical exposure with diverse patient population, though UAE may have more advanced facilities and higher salaries. Oman excels in work-life balance and cost of living.

**Q: Can my family accompany me to Oman?**

Yes, once you meet minimum salary requirements (typically OMR 600-1,000), you can sponsor your spouse and children for residence visas.
